Helpdesk Ticketing System

Released in November 2009, our custom helpdesk is a vital tool used by Site5 staff! Written in PHP, it was designed to mimic the feel and operations of the larger, well-known commercial and open source support ticket systems. We wanted and needed custom functions, abilities, and reporting available to management, but we quickly learned what we wanted wasn't available!

So what do we do? Of course we write our own!

Our helpdesk handles thousands of replies daily between staff and customers. It's vital nature means development work will never stop; there will always be something we want it to do (or not do!), and that's exactly why we love writing our own software. We don't like something, we change it. Easy as that.
