Backstage Account Manager

Backstage is our customer account manager written entirely in Ruby on Rails. First released in February 2006, it has undergone numerous changes and updates as any good app does! The last major revision to Backstage was released in tandem with SiteAdmin in mid-2011.

Among Backstage's features:

  • Extremely close integration with SiteAdmin
  • Ability to create additional users each with their own specific permissions
  • Manage all billing aspects of an account
  • Register new domains and manage existing domain registrations
  • Create, respond to, and view support tickets
  • Full-fledged affiliate system
