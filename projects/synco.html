Synco CRM & Billing System

Synco is our internal customer resource management and billing system. Built using Ruby on Rails, Synco and its client-facing counterpart Backstage are used by every staff member and client. From the order process to provisioning of orders to cancellations, Synco handles it all!

First released in February 2006, Synco has seen many changes and improvements over the years. Though it has served Site5 extremely well, the decision was made to begin a complete rewrite from the ground up. Many of the underlying technologies have evolved since Synco's creation, and after much deliberation, we came to the conclusion that a complete rewrite was the best solution.

Expected v1 release of Synco 2.0 is slated for December 2011/January 2012.
