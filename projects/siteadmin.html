SiteAdmin Control Panel

SiteAdmin is our custom cPanel™ theme developed exclusively for Site5 web hosting customers. It's written in PHP and utilizes ajax extensively. Formally known as NetAdmin, SiteAdmin was originally released in 2000. Our revision released in mid-2011 brought it to version 2.0.

Among SiteAdmin's features:

  • Extremely close integration with Backstage
  • Ajax modal windows to reduce the number of required page loads
  • Manage all billing aspects of an account
  • Register new domains and manage existing domain registrations
  • Create, respond to, and view support tickets
  • Full-fledged affiliate system
